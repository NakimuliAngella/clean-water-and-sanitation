ABOUT CLEANWATER INITIATIVE

Transforming lives through sustainable water solutions since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, CleanWater Initiative began with a simple mission: to provide access to clean, safe drinking water for communities in need. What started as a small group of volunteers has grown into an international organization serving over 1.2 million people across 15 countries.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to eliminating waterborne diseases and improving quality of life through sustainable water sanitation projects, education, and community empowerment.

Our Vision

A world where every person has access to clean water and proper sanitation facilities, regardless of their location or economic status.

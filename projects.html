OUR WATER PROJECTS

Transforming communities through sustainable water solutions

Clean Water Systems

Installation of advanced water filtration systems in rural villages across East Africa. This project provides safe drinking water to over 50,000 people.

  • Location: Kenya, Tanzania
  • Beneficiaries: 50,000+ people
  • Status: Ongoing
  • Completion: 85%

Safe Water Wells

Drilling and maintenance of sustainable water wells providing long-term access to clean water in remote communities.

  • Location: Multiple countries
  • Wells Built: 500+
  • Status: Active
  • Maintenance: Ongoing

Healthy Lives Program

Education and sanitation programs to prevent waterborne diseases and promote hygiene practices in schools and communities.

  • Location: South Asia, Africa
  • Schools Reached: 200+
  • Status: Expanding
  • Impact: 100,000+ children

Emergency Water Response

Rapid deployment of water purification units and sanitation facilities in disaster-affected areas and refugee camps.

  • Location: Crisis zones worldwide
  • Response Time: 24-48 hours
  • Status: On-call
  • Capacity: 10,000 people/day
